Color Synth Lite

A lightweight offline tool for hex color experiments

Usage

  • Enter values in Hex, RGB, or HSV; hit Update to preview.
  • Use Alchemy Lab for channel swaps and midpoints between colors.
  • Click the preview square to copy hex.
